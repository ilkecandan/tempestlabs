Install FunnelFlow as a PWA

For the best experience, install FunnelFlow as a Progressive Web App on your device:

iPhone/iPad

  1. Open in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button
  3. Select "Add to Home Screen"

Android

  1. Open in Chrome
  2. Tap the 3-dot menu
  3. Select "Install App"

Desktop

  1. Chrome/Edge: Look for install icon in address bar
  2. Firefox: Menu > "Install"
  3. Safari: File > "Add to Dock"

Why Did My Payment Fail?

Several factors could have prevented your payment from processing:

  • Bank restrictions: Some banks block international transactions by default
  • Insufficient funds: Check your account balance
  • Card limits: Daily/monthly transaction limits may apply
  • Browser issues: Try a different browser or disable ad blockers
  • Currency conversion: Your bank may decline foreign currency charges
  • 3D Secure: Your bank's additional verification may have failed
