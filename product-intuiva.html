How Intuiva Works

Intuiva's unique backward planning approach helps you visualize success first, then builds the path to get there. Here's the process:

  1. Define Your Vision

    Start by describing your end goal in simple terms. Intuiva's AI will ask thoughtful questions to clarify your vision and identify potential blind spots.

  2. Generate Your Strategy

    Receive a customized project blueprint with key milestones, risk factors, and resource requirementsâ€”all tailored to your specific goals.

  3. Work Backwards

    Set your launch date or target milestone, and Intuiva will reverse-engineer the perfect timeline with all necessary steps automatically scheduled.

  4. Execute with Confidence

    Follow your personalized Kanban board with AI-generated tasks that adapt as you progress. Intuiva learns from your work patterns and adjusts future recommendations.

Pro Tip: The Power of Backward Planning

Traditional planning starts with what you know today and moves forward linearly. This often leads to missed steps and unrealistic timelines. Intuiva's backward planning begins with your desired outcome and works backward to today, ensuring every critical step is included and properly sequenced.

This method, used by elite athletes and top CEOs, creates more accurate plans and helps identify dependencies early in the process.
