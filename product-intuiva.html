How Intuiva Works

Intuiva is your lean, thinking assistant—designed to help solo founders, creatives, and teams plan smarter without the clutter. It guides you from vision to execution with structure, not stress.

  1. 1. Set Up Your Project

    Create your account and start instantly. You'll land in the Project Planner, where the journey begins.

  2. 2. Answer Targeted AI Questions

    Intuiva asks sharp, focused questions about your project—your goals, users, risks, and timeline. You can skip or revisit any part. The goal? Clarity without overwhelm.

  3. 3. Receive Your Strategy Blueprint

    Once your answers are in, Intuiva generates a customized project plan. It includes a summary, key milestones, risks with mitigation ideas, and tactical suggestions—all beautifully presented and downloadable as a PDF.

  4. 4. Visualize It with Flow Boards

    Your plan flows into an interactive Kanban board called Flow Boards. Each task is placed into default columns like "To Do," "In Progress," and "Done"—but you can create custom stages, drag and drop, delete, or expand tasks freely.

  5. 5. Export, Save, and Share

    Want to send updates or archive your progress? You can export both the report and your Flow Board as PDFs. Generate shareable read-only links for teammates or clients.

  6. 6. Optional: Use Backcasting Mode

    If you prefer to start from a clear outcome—like "launch in July" or "user test by week 4"—Intuiva can walk you backwards from that endpoint. It calculates key checkpoints and gives you a reverse timeline.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Intuiva and how it can transform your planning process.

How does Intuiva differ from traditional project management tools?

Traditional tools like Trello or Asana are task managers—they help you organize work but don't help you plan it. Intuiva is a project strategist that helps you determine what work needs to be done in the first place. Our AI analyzes your goals and generates a customized plan with all necessary steps, then helps you execute it.

Unlike rigid Gantt charts or Kanban boards, Intuiva adapts to your thinking style and provides intelligent suggestions based on your progress and changing priorities.

Can I share my projects with others?

Yes! You can generate shareable read-only links for any of your Flow Boards. This allows stakeholders to view your progress without making changes. For team collaboration, each member would need their own Intuiva license.

How is my data secured?

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for all data at rest and in transit. Your account is protected with secure authentication, and we regularly audit our systems for vulnerabilities.

What platforms does Intuiva support?

Intuiva is available as:

  • A web app (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
  • Windows and macOS desktop apps
  • iOS and Android mobile apps
  • A Progressive Web App (PWA) for offline use

All versions sync seamlessly when you're online. The mobile apps are particularly popular for capturing ideas on the go.

How often do you release updates?

We release minor updates every 2-3 weeks with performance improvements and bug fixes. Major feature updates come every 3-4 months based on user feedback.

All updates are free for existing customers—there are no upgrade fees or "version 2.0" paywalls. When you buy Intuiva, you own it for life.
