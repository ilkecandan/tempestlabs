How Intuiva Works

  1. Start a Project

    Kick off by outlining your idea. Intuiva will ask a few AI-powered questions (you can skip any if you like) to help refine your vision. You get instant insights with every answer, so you’re never planning in the dark.

  2. Get an AI-Generated Summary

    Receive a custom summary report of your project’s strategy. Intuiva compiles your goals, key steps, and potential risks into a beautifully structured PDF that you can download or share for feedback.

  3. View Your Kanban Board

    All your tasks are instantly organized into an interactive Kanban board. Customize it as you see fit: drag-and-drop tasks, add new columns (ideas, feedback, etc.), and mark progress as you go. You can even share a read-only link so others can follow along with your progress.

  4. Plan Backwards from the Future

    Set a future goal or launch date, and Intuiva will work backward to build your success map. It auto-generates milestones from the future back to today, giving you a step-by-step timeline. Edit and adjust these milestones until the plan fits your schedule.
