Why Intuiva?

Intuiva is your AI-powered project strategist, designed for visionary thinkers, entrepreneurs, and innovators who need a structured yet flexible way to turn ideas into reality. Unlike traditional project tools, Intuiva helps you see the future and work backward to make it happen.

How Intuiva Works

1. Start a Project

Answer AI-powered questions or skip them—your choice. Receive instant insights with every step.

2. Get AI-Generated Summary

Receive a custom summary of your project. Download it as a beautifully structured PDF.

3. View Your Kanban Board

Your tasks are ready to go. Customize, drag and drop, add columns, and share read-only views.

4. Plan Backwards from the Future

Set your future goal, and Intuiva works backwards—building your success map with editable milestones.
