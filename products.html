Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and policies.

Are your products available on mobile?
Currently, our products are desktop-only (Windows and macOS). We're exploring mobile versions for future releases.
How often do you release updates?
We release major updates quarterly with new features, and minor updates as needed for bug fixes and improvements. All updates are free for existing customers.
Do I need an account to use your apps?
Nope. Most of our tools are designed for minimal friction. Just buy, enter your PIN, and go.
How does the one-time payment work?
You pay once, receive a secure PIN, and unlock the app forever. No subscriptions, no renewals, no catches.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes—if something doesn't work or wasn't what you expected, reach out within 7 days. We'll make it right.
Why no subscriptions? Isn't that bad for business?
Not for us. We believe software should empower, not trap. Subscriptions are great—for gyms you forget to cancel.

Our tools are one-time purchase because that's what respect looks like in code form. We design with intention, price with integrity, and trust you'll support what's truly useful.
Where are you based?
Geographically? Between Turkey, Switzerland, somewhere in Europe and a good Wi-Fi connection.

Philosophically? Somewhere between an ancient library and a futuristic observatory—where myths meet machines and clarity is the currency.
How do I install your apps on iOS? (Because Apple doesn't make it obvious)
Great question, and yes—our apps are Progressive Web Apps (PWAs). That means they behave like regular apps, without App Store chaos.

To install on iPhone or iPad:

1. Open the app link in Safari (not Chrome).
2. Tap the Share icon (the little square with an arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen."
4. Voilà! It now lives on your screen like any other app—except calmer.

(We wish Apple made this easier. But until then, we whisper magic instructions.)
