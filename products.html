How does the one-time payment work?

You pay once and own it forever. That's it. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no nonsense. All future updates are included at no additional cost.

We believe in simple, transparent pricing that respects our customers. Unlike subscription models that lock you into ongoing payments, our one-time purchase gives you lifetime access to the software.

Do your apps work offline?

Absolutely. All our apps are designed to work completely offline. Internet is only required for initial download and optional cloud sync features.

Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to sync it across devices using our secure cloud service. We prioritize your privacy and accessibility.

What platforms do you support?

We currently support Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android. Our apps are optimized for each platform while maintaining a consistent experience across devices.

When you purchase one of our products, you get access to all platform versions with a single license. Your data syncs seamlessly between devices when you enable cloud sync.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee with no questions asked. If our product doesn't meet your expectations, we'll refund your purchase.

We're confident you'll love our software, but we want you to try it risk-free. Just contact our support team within 7 days of purchase for a full refund.

How often do you release updates?

We release minor updates monthly with bug fixes and small improvements. Major feature updates come out quarterly based on user feedback.

Our development roadmap is heavily influenced by customer requests. When you suggest a feature, our team evaluates it for potential inclusion in future updates.

What are the system requirements?

Our apps are designed to be lightweight and efficient:

  • Windows: Windows 10 or later, 4GB RAM, 200MB disk space
  • macOS: macOS 10.14 or later, 4GB RAM, 200MB disk space
  • iOS: iOS 14 or later, iPhone 8 or newer
  • Android: Android 9 or later, 3GB RAM
Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! We offer a 40% discount for students and educators. Just contact our support team with proof of your academic status (student ID, enrollment verification, or faculty email) and we'll provide a discount code.

We believe in supporting the next generation of creators and thinkers with affordable access to our tools.
