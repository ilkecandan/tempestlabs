INTUIVA AS PWA

Intuiva can be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your device for a native app-like experience with these benefits:

  • Offline functionality - access some features without internet
  • Faster loading times and smoother performance
  • Home screen icon just like a regular app
  • Push notifications for important updates

HOW TO INSTALL:

  1. Open Intuiva in Chrome or Safari
  2. Look for the "Add to Home Screen" prompt or find it in the browser menu
  3. Confirm the installation
  4. Launch Intuiva from your home screen
