✨ What Is a Marketing Funnel?

A marketing funnel is the journey every customer takes before buying. It has stages like:

Awareness

Interest

Intent

Evaluation

Purchase

Each stage has its own psychology. Most businesses focus on the finish line. The smart ones focus on the steps. FunnelFlow helps you see the full path and control every part of it.
