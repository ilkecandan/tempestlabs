The Five Stages of Flow

Every customer journey follows these stages. FunnelFlow helps you visualize and optimize each one.

Understand Your Customer's Path

Awareness: They discover your brand exists through ads, content, or word of mouth.

Interest: They engage with your content, visit your site, or follow you.

Intent: They show signs of wanting what you offer—saving items, signing up.

Evaluation: They compare you to alternatives, read reviews, consider options.

Purchase: They decide to buy from you and complete the transaction.

FunnelFlow helps you see exactly where prospects get stuck and what content moves them forward.
