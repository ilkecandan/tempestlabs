Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FunnelFlow

Is FunnelFlow a CRM?

No, FunnelFlow complements your CRM by providing the visualization layer most CRMs lack. It's designed to work alongside tools like HubSpot or Salesforce, helping you see the bigger picture of your marketing funnel.

How do I get my data into FunnelFlow?

FunnelFlow allows you to manually add potential leads to your funnels. While we don't connect to external data sources, you can easily export your funnel data to Excel for further analysis.

How much does it cost?

During our launch period, FunnelFlow is just $9.99 (one-time payment). This includes all core features and updates. No monthly fees, no upsells.

Is there a learning curve?

FunnelFlow is designed for marketers, not developers. If you can use tools like Trello or Notion, you'll feel right at home. We provide template funnels and step-by-step guidance to get you started quickly.
