Frequently Asked Questions

Is FunnelFlow a CRM?

No, FunnelFlow is not a traditional CRM. While CRMs focus on contact management and sales pipelines, FunnelFlow specializes in visualizing and optimizing your entire marketing funnel. It's designed to work alongside your existing CRM, providing the visualization layer that most CRMs lack.

How does the visualization help my marketing?

Visualizing your funnel provides several key benefits:

  • Immediately see where prospects are dropping off
  • Understand which content performs best at each stage
  • Identify bottlenecks in your customer journey
  • Make data-driven decisions about where to focus improvements
  • Align your team around a shared understanding of the funnel

Our customers typically see a 30-200% improvement in conversion rates after implementing FunnelFlow.

Can I use it for multiple projects?

Yes! Your license allows you to use FunnelFlow for as many projects as you need. Many of our customers manage multiple funnels for different products, services, or client accounts all within the same dashboard.

Do I need technical skills to use it?

Not at all. FunnelFlow is designed for marketers, not developers. If you can use tools like Trello or Notion, you'll feel right at home with FunnelFlow. We provide step-by-step onboarding and template funnels to get you started quickly.
