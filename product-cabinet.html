You contain multitudes. All of them deserve a voice.

Cabinet of Selves is a beautifully designed journaling and self-therapy app with an AI companion, helping you explore, understand, and give voice to every part of your inner world—privately, gently, and without judgment.

Start your journey now

See & Name Your Inner World

Explore the diverse voices within you—protectors, exiles, artists, dreamers. Give them names. Assign them images. Let each part take up space in your story and find peace in being seen.

Journaling & Daily Check-Ins

Track your internal weather. Journal freely or follow reflective prompts. Identify which parts are active and how they influence your emotions, choices, and relationships over time.

Talk to an AI Companion

Have thoughtful, guided conversations with an AI assistant designed to mirror your thoughts, not judge them. It’s not therapy—but it is a space to explore with curiosity and kindness.

Private & Offline

No logins. No accounts. No data sent anywhere. Cabinet of Selves runs entirely on your device. Your mind is sacred—your data stays yours.
