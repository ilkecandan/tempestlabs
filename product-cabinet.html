Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MindScape

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption to ensure your data remains private. Your journal entries and mood logs are only accessible to you, and we never sell or share your data with third parties.

How does the AI analysis work?

Our AI analyzes your journal entries for emotional tone, recurring themes, and potential triggers. It runs entirely on your device (for mobile apps) or in your browser (web version), so your data never leaves your possession.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export all your data at any time in multiple formats (PDF, CSV, JSON). We believe you should always have full control over your information.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! MindScape is available on both iOS and Android, with full sync capabilities between web and mobile. All your data stays up-to-date across all devices.
