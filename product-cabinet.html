Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Cabinet of Selves

What is Internal Family Systems (IFS)?

IFS is a therapeutic approach that views the mind as made up of multiple sub-personalities or "parts" that interact like a family. The model helps people understand and heal their inner world by developing relationships with these parts and accessing their core Self.

Is this a substitute for therapy?

No, Cabinet of Selves is not therapy. It's a self-help tool that can complement therapy or be used for personal growth. For mental health concerns, we recommend consulting with a licensed professional.

How is this different from journaling?

While journaling is valuable, our tool provides specific frameworks from IFS to structure your inner exploration. The visualizations and guided exercises help you see patterns and relationships between parts that might be harder to recognize through traditional journaling.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never sell your data. You can export or delete your information at any time. Your inner exploration remains private.

What if I don't understand my parts?

The tool includes guided exercises to help you identify and understand your parts. Many people find that with regular use, their understanding naturally deepens over time. There's no "wrong" way to experience your inner world.
