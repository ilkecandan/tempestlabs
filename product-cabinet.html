The Science of Selves

Internal Family Systems (IFS) is an evidence-based psychotherapy approach developed by Dr. Richard Schwartz in the 1980s. It recognizes the mind as naturally multiple - composed of sub-personalities, each with valuable qualities.

IFS therapy helps individuals access their Self - the calm, curious, compassionate center of consciousness that can heal wounded parts and restore inner harmony.

Cabinet of Selves adapts these clinical insights into a structured self-discovery tool to help you map your internal system independent of therapy.

Important: This is not a therapy alternative. For clinical issues, we recommend working with an IFS-trained therapist.

The Power of Visualization

Visualization is a powerful therapeutic tool that bridges conscious and unconscious processes, enabling deeper self-understanding and integration.

Self-Concept Discovery

Visualization helps individuals mentally simulate different scenarios, understand various aspects of self, and integrate them. By creating mental images of our internal parts, we can interact with them more directly and compassionately.

Therapeutic Techniques

Practices like guided imagery help individuals confront and reconcile conflicting parts of identity, developing a harmonious self-concept. Research shows visualization activates the same neural networks as real experiences, making it a powerful tool for psychological change.

Psychological Guidance

Answers to your therapeutic questions

Is this a therapy alternative?

No. Cabinet of Selves is designed as a self-help tool based on therapeutic principles, but does not replace professional mental health services. For clinical issues, we recommend working with an IFS-trained therapist.

Important Note: We are not therapists. This tool complements therapy, it doesn't replace it.

How is this different from journaling?

While journaling is valuable, our tool offers structured clinical protocols from IFS therapy. Mapping and visualization tools help you see systemic relationships between parts that traditional journaling might miss.

We also use DeepSeek's AI API to provide insights while protecting your privacy - all processing happens locally on your device.

Is my psychological data secure?

Absolutely. We use a local storage only approach - your data never leaves your device. No user profiles or accounts are created. You can export your work as PDF for your records or to share with your therapist.

For additional security, the app includes an option to completely delete all local data at any time.

What if I encounter difficult emotions?

The tool includes grounding exercises and pacing protocols. However, if you encounter overwhelming material, we recommend pausing your work and consulting a mental health professional before continuing.

Remember: This is a self-discovery tool, not a therapeutic intervention. Proceed at your own pace.

Can I use this alongside therapy?

Many therapists recommend using Cabinet of Selves to enhance clinical work. You can share your maps and insights with your therapist to enrich your sessions. Always follow your therapist's guidance.

We specifically provide PDF export for this purpose - to facilitate communication between you and your mental health professional.
