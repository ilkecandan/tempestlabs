Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Cabinet of Selves

Is this therapy or a mental health treatment?

No, Cabinet of Selves is not therapy or a mental health treatment. It's a self-help tool designed to support emotional awareness and personal reflection. For mental health concerns, we recommend consulting with a licensed professional.

Can this replace my therapy sessions?

Cabinet of Selves is not a replacement for therapy. It's designed to be a supplementary tool that some people find helpful alongside professional care. We always recommend following your mental health provider's advice.

How is this different from journaling?

While journaling is valuable, our tool adds structure by helping you track patterns over time and offering guided prompts based on psychological concepts. The visualizations can help you see connections you might miss in traditional journaling.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never sell your data. You can export or delete your information at any time. Your reflections are for your eyes only.

What if I don't have time for daily check-ins?

The daily check-in is designed to take just 2-3 minutes. Many users do it while having their morning coffee or before bed. Even occasional use can provide valuable insights, though regular use tends to be most beneficial.
