Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Cabinet of Selves

What is Internal Family Systems (IFS) therapy?

IFS is a therapeutic approach that views the mind as made up of multiple sub-personalities or "parts" that interact like a family. These parts can be protective, wounded, or serve other roles. The goal is to help these parts work harmoniously under the leadership of your core Self.

Is Cabinet of Selves a replacement for therapy?

No, Cabinet of Selves is designed to complement therapy, not replace it. Many users find it enhances their therapeutic work by providing tools for self-reflection between sessions. For serious mental health concerns, we always recommend working with a licensed professional.

How does the AI companion work?

Our AI companion uses natural language processing to help you explore your thoughts and feelings. It's trained to ask thoughtful questions and reflect your words back to help you gain insight. It doesn't diagnose or give professional advice, but rather serves as a compassionate listener.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and never sell your data. You own all your content and can export or delete it at any time. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Can I use Cabinet of Selves with my therapist?

Yes! Many therapists appreciate when clients use our tools between sessions. The Professional plan even includes features specifically designed for therapist-client collaboration.
