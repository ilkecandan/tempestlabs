About Tempest Labs

At Tempest Labs, we build digital products that bring clarity, focus, and depth to modern work and life. Our vision is to create tools that empower independent thinkers, helping them structure ideas, manage projects, and make informed decisions—without unnecessary clutter or distractions.

We prioritize elegant design, seamless functionality, and meaningful experiences over trends. Our products are built for those who value depth over noise, simplicity over excess, and quality over quick fixes.

Tempest Labs was founded by İlke Candan Bengi, a researcher and developer with a background in materials science, AI, and web technologies. Driven by the belief that technology should serve, not overwhelm, the company focuses on crafting tools that integrate effortlessly into daily life.

Our approach is simple: No subscriptions, no complexity—just well-made, one-time purchase digital tools that work.
