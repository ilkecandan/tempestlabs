About Tempest Labs

We build clarity-first digital tools for modern minds navigating chaos

Tempest Labs builds clarity-first digital tools for modern minds navigating chaos.

Our mission is simple:
Help you think deeply, work deliberately, and reclaim focus—without clutter, complexity, or subscriptions.

We craft minimalist, one-time purchase products that structure your ideas, guide your projects, and protect your mental space. No pings. No paywalls. Just tools that work, beautifully.

We prioritize:

  • Design that breathes
  • Functionality that flows
  • Experiences that matter

Tempest Labs was founded by İlke Candan Bengi, a researcher and developer with a background in materials science, AI, and web technologies. Her work bridges logic and feeling, form and function—infused with the belief that technology should serve your mind, not hijack it.
